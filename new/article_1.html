OPEN LETTER TO HIS MAJESTY KING MSWATI III

OPEN LETTER TO HIS MAJESTY KING MSWATI III

08/07/2021

Your Majesty, you will forgive me for addressing you on this platform that is Facebook but it is the only way I can try to get my message to you. I am writing this as a very concerned citizen and father. Our country has become a failed state and is making headlines for the wrong reasons, everyday things are getting worse.

I have always had a deep love for my country Swaziland and the swazi culture as a whole of which I still do as it is my identity. I grew up Ezulwini KaMasina Ebuka. Growing up the King was my hero watching him on a show called Tasebukhosini. This inspired me at an early age to want to join the regiment and attend Lusekwane. I have attended Lusekwane and Incwala since the tender age of 10 until recently in 2019. I take pride in my culture and everywhere I have travelled and stayed I have always promoted and let everyone know that mine ngiliswati phaca.

Your Majesty, as one of the youth of this kingdom I know one too many that I share sentiments with about the future of this kingdom, it is our only country after all and we love it. In 1973, my simple and basic understanding is that HRH King Sobhuza ii, implemented the 1973 decree as a means to protecting the country from being hijacked through political parties as a vast majority of Emaswati were illiterate and did not understand what a democracy was. Over the years, as Emaswati we have become a well-educated nation that understands and knows what type of democracy would suit us as a Nation.

This call was not and is not a fight against the Ingwenyama yeMaswati, it is a rallying call for all Emaswati and our king himself as a member of our community and he too as a legitimate sovereign would want his Nation to progress and prosper, thus this is not a fight against the king but a fight against the current dispensation. We as the youth and people of this kingdom in conjunction with the King would like to work towards a country that is democratic, free and fair in all aspects of our society . We currently have many a number of emaSwati who are in the diaspora successfully running the affairs of other nations, making them prosper and developed. Sesidziniwe kuba khashane nasekhaya. Having learned about what a 1st world country is, should we as a nation be granted this right, even the Kings Vision2022 for a 1st world Eswatini will be fulfilled.

Kute ke indvodza nome make longavuma Loko your Majesty. Kungako live lidvungutela kangaka. Asisakhoni kubeka lipalishi etafuleni. Inyama keyona awu asisayati.

Nyalo eveni kutsiwa Mswati udla imali nebantfwana bakhe, natsi siyakubona vele ngoba kwenteka embhikwetfu. Kungulokunye lokufaka liswayi esilondzeni nawuhlupheka uzama, kungahlangani kodvwa ubona labanye baphumelela kodvwa ungaboni kutsi yini lephilako labayentako?.

Your Majesty, as insha yeMaswati sicela usive, usilalele ungavumeli sibulalane sodvwa ngoba sonkhe laveni simunye, sikhuluma lulwimi lunye asikho sidzingo sekutsi Hulumende aphendvule ngesibhuku esiveni sakhe. Sifundzile, sikhulile futsi sonkhe sifisile kubona lelive leMaswati lichubekela phambili. I pray and hope this letter reaches where it is meant to.
