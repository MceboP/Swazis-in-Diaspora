by Mfomfo Nkhambule

Let us be clear on this multi-party democracy.

04/08/2021

Most of those advocating for multi-party democracy have very little knowledge of democracy because no one in this generation has been exposed to it. It has been frown at by the powers that be since donkey years ago. It has been demonised by those who are close to the King because they know the consequences of tilting the bar towards the democracy side. It would be disastrous for them and their families.

The only experience Swazis may have on democracy is from Google . The determination of the Swazis to try out something they feel could be much better than the status quo is a sign of desperation and frustration at the same time. This generation of young men and women want anything but Tinkhundla because Tinkhundla has not allowed them to be involved in key decision making structures.

There is nothing as demoralising than to be labelled an idiot without having been given a chance to demonstrate capacity to resolve certain challenges .

There are different versions of democracies which include unfamiliar brands like African democracy where the people are always having a leader who decides for many people who have no say whatsoever in the affairs of the country from governance to judiciary. The leader is never questioned on the decisions taken because he surrounds himself with â€œyes" men. This kind of system has no checks and balances to avoid situations of abuse of office powers.

The other one is the participatory version where emphasis is on structures and the role of the citizens to be decision makers through certain structures that they have agreed upon via the justice system that is easily accessible to all and protects the dignity of a man. In this one there is no room for people who are good at manipulating the ignorant masses.
