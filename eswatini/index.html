About Swazis in Diaspora

Swazis In Diaspora is an independent social movement whose main objective is to tell the stories and share experiences of the people of Eswatini around the world and within the country itself.

We are a non-partisan social body that advocates for a democratic Eswatini. Our approach is people centered and encourages the people of Eswatini to tell their story and own their experiences.

It is true that in conflict situations, the first casualty is the truth. Our platform provides an opportunity for all people and friends of Eswatini, within and outside the borders of the kingdom, to share through written articles, photography or video footage, their experiences of current political situation in the small kingdom.
